Generative model

Transmission chains: $\mathbf{C} = (c_1, \ldots, c_K)$

Sequence clusters: $\mathbf{S} = (s_1, \ldots, s_K)$

Binomial sampling: $P(s_i \, | \, c_i, \psi) = \binom{c_i}{s_i} \psi^s_i (1-\psi)^{c_i-s_i}$
